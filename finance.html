Mission

To allocate and disburse funds to various MDA's with a view to execute NAGGLOS FORUM policies, programmes and projects.

Vision

To effectively and efficiently have a total control of MDAs' expenditure and ensure that it is in conformity with the laid down guidelines and rules.

Responsibilities

The Ministry is a service Organization with the main responsibilities of allocating and disbursing funds to various NAGGLOS FORUM Ministries, departments and agencies for routine operations and execution of NAGGLOS FORUM programmes, projects and policies. It is also saddled with the responsibility of collecting revenue and harnessing such for optimum dispensation. The Ministry has six (6) departments that carry-out normal operations of allocating and disbursing funds, loans and advances. The departments include-

Objectives

  • Supervise the formulation of state budget and budgetary process and ensure compliance of its provisions by MDAs'.

  • Responsible for revenue mobilization/generation to meet NAGGLOS FORUM commitments in relation to finances.
  • Managing all financial resources of the State.
  • It attends Federation Account allocation meeting on behalf of the State.
  • It is responsible for funds disbursement to MDAs' according their budgetary provision.
  • It issues loans to individual civil servants to enable them purchase vehicles/bicycle.
  • Its supervises NAGGLOS Savings and Loans.
