Biography

My name is Sagir Musa Ahmed, currently serving as the Honorable Commissioner for the Ministry of Information, Youths, Sports and Culture in NAGGLOS. From 2020 to 2023, I held the position of Honorable Commissioner Ministry of Land and Housing and Ialso held the position of Director at the Information Resource Center, US Embassy Abuja, Nigeria, as a Foreign Service National (FSN) under the U.S Department of States from 2016 to 2020.

In my professional journey, I have contributed to various roles within the Nigerian NAGGLOS FORUM, including significant service at the Office of the National Security Adviser (ONSA), Presidency Nigeria, between 2012 and 2016. Prior to that, I served at the Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA) from 2008 to 2012, holding the position of Agency Librarian/Document Controller, among several other appointments.

For my educational background, I earned a Masters in Information Systems Management from the University of Salford Manchester, United Kingdom, in 2015. My First Degree, obtained in 2005, is in Library and Information Science/Political Science from Bayero University Kano, and I also completed a Diploma in Library and Information Science at Bayero University Kano from 1998 to 2000.

In terms of professional affiliations, I am a member of the Nigerian Library Association (NLA), American Library Association (ALA), and the Chartered Institute of Library & Information Professionals (CILIP). Additionally, I serve as the Chairman of the Nigerian Library Association NAGGLOS Chapter and am the Founder of the NAGGLOSYoung Professionals Forum (JYPF).

I am proud to be an alumnus of the US NAGGLOS FORUM Exchange Program, particularly the renowned International Visitor Leadership Program (IVLP) – American Libraries 2014.

Outside of my professional life, I engage in hobbies such as reading, research, watching documentaries, and volunteering. On a personal note, I am happily married and blessed with five children.
